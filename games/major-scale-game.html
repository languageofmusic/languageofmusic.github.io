Note guessing game

How to play:

Step 1:
Click "Play Scale", to familiarize yourself with the sound of the scale. You will here notes 1 to 8 of a major scale (8 is the same as 1, just octave higher)

Step 2:
Click "Play Random Note" to hear a random note between 1 and 8 from the scale you just heard.

Step 3:
Guess which note you just heard by clicking the numbers. If you get it right, click "New Random Note". If you get it wrong, you can click "Play Random Note" to hear the same note again, and make another guess.

Have fun!
